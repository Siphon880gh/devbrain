
|Area|Main Job|
|---|---|
|**Data Engineering**|Builds the pipelines, databases, warehouses, lakes, and infrastructure that move, clean, transform, and organize data|
|**Data Analysis**|Looks at data to answer business questions|
|**Data Science**|Uses statistics, machine learning, and modeling to make predictions or discover patterns|
|**Business Analytics**|Uses data to improve business decisions, strategy, operations, and performance|
|**Business Intelligence**|Creates dashboards, reports, KPIs, and recurring visibility into the business|

Data engineering is the technical foundation that prepares and delivers data for data analysis, business intelligence, business analytics, and data science.

The relationship is like:
```
Data Engineering        ↓Creates reliable data foundation        ↓Data Analysis / BI / Business Analytics / Data Science
```
