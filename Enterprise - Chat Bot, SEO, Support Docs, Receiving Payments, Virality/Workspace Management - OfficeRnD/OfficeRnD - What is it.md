[OfficeRnD](https://www.officernd.com/) is a workspace management platform for **hybrid offices, coworking spaces, and flexible work environments**.

It helps teams manage desk booking, meeting rooms, visitors, member accounts, billing, access control, workplace analytics, and space usage.

OfficeRnD mainly serves two groups:

- **OfficeRnD Workplace** — for companies managing hybrid work and shared office space.
- **OfficeRnD Flex** — for coworking spaces and flex-space operators.

---

## By Industry

### Higher Education

**Hybrid workspace management for academia**

OfficeRnD helps universities and colleges manage shared offices, study spaces, meeting rooms, research spaces, and hybrid campus work environments.

### Healthcare

**Smart space scheduling for care teams**

Healthcare teams can use OfficeRnD to schedule shared workspaces, admin offices, meeting rooms, and collaboration areas across changing staff schedules.

### Tech Companies

**Hybrid work platform for engineering teams**

Tech companies use OfficeRnD for desk hoteling, room booking, team scheduling, hybrid work coordination, and office usage tracking.

### Financial Services

**Compliant workspace management for finance**

Financial services teams can use OfficeRnD to manage desks, rooms, visitors, access, and workspace policies in a more organized and controlled way.

### Flex and Coworking Spaces

**All-in-one software for coworking operators**

OfficeRnD Flex helps coworking operators manage memberships, bookings, billing, contracts, payments, member apps, visitors, reports, and multi-location operations.

## Simple Summary

OfficeRnD is for any organization that needs to manage **who uses a space, when they use it, how it gets booked, and how the space performs over time**.
