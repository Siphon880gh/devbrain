You are a senior prompt engineer and AI workflow strategist.

I am going to paste a prompt from someone else’s prompt library. I do **not** want to blindly copy and run it. I want you to help me understand it, adapt it, and use it correctly.

Your job is to analyze the prompt like an AI engineer would.

## What I want you to do

### 1. Explain the purpose of the prompt

Tell me:

- What is this prompt trying to accomplish?
- What kind of output is it designed to produce?
- What type of user or business use case is it best for?
- Is it meant for strategy, content creation, analysis, automation, sales, marketing, research, coding, or something else?

### 2. Break down the prompt structure

Identify the major parts of the prompt, such as:

- Role
- Goal
- Context
- Input requirements
- Rules or constraints
- Output format
- Examples
- Step-by-step process
- Follow-up behavior

Explain why each part matters.

### 3. Identify what inputs I need to provide

Tell me what information I should prepare before using this prompt.

For example:

- Product or service
- Target audience
- Industry
- Offer
- Pricing
- Brand voice
- Desired output format
- Examples
- Constraints
- Geographic market
- Business goal

Also tell me which inputs are required, optional, or missing.

### 4. Explain how I should interact with the AI when using this prompt

Tell me whether this prompt is designed for:

- A one-shot answer
- A back-and-forth conversation
- Step-by-step refinement
- A multi-stage workflow
- A reusable template

Then explain how I should guide the AI after the first response.

For example:

- What should I ask next?
- What should I revise?
- What should I test?
- What should I clarify?
- What should I watch out for?

### 5. Point out weaknesses or risks

Look for problems such as:

- Vague instructions
- Missing context
- Overly broad goals
- Unclear output format
- Assumptions that may not apply to me
- Risk of hallucinated facts
- Instructions that conflict with each other
- Parts that are too advanced, unnecessary, or hard to use

### 6. Recommend improvements

Give me a better version of the prompt if needed.

Make the improved version:

- Clearer
- More practical
- Easier to reuse
- Better structured
- More specific about inputs and outputs
- Safer against fake facts or generic answers

### 7. Give me a usage guide

At the end, give me:

- A short summary of what this prompt does
- The best way to use it
- The exact inputs I should fill in
- Suggested follow-up questions I can ask after running it
- A final improved version of the prompt, if appropriate

## Important

Do not just praise the prompt. Critique it honestly.

Do not rewrite it immediately without explaining how it works first.

Do not assume the prompt is already good.

Think like an AI engineer reviewing another AI engineer’s work.
