For a small website or personal project, the email included with hosting is usually fine. (Eg. Cpanel comes included with email)

For business-critical email, cold outreach, client communication, invoices, password resets, or anything where deliverability matters, I would usually use:

```
Google Workspace
Microsoft 365
Zoho
MailProton Mail Business
Fastmail
```

The reason is simple: **cheap hosting email may work, but deliverability and spam reputation can be weaker**, especially on shared hosting where many customers share the same infrastructure.
