Required knowledge:
- Many web hosting companies offer both web-host service and email service, usually as independent services you subscribe to.

When you might want to migrate to another email service:
- You just migrated web hosting, and you prefer having email service on your new web host so it's less accounts to juggle
- Email service price has increased compared to competitors. However, don't just go for the cheapest service because you should also consider deliverability (more on the next point)
- You looked up deliverability and it's no longer as reliable. Redditors may talk about deliverability as outbound emails vs as just a business email, so match your use case to the deliverability. You can look up by googling `reddit __service_name__ deliverability` and keeping the time scoped to the last year.
- You want to move to Google workspace where there's integration with other Google tools like Google Drive
