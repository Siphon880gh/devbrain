Go to manage your Google account:
http://myaccount.google.com/

The UX sucks as of 5/2025. Just search for "app password". It is usually NOT the first option:
![[Pasted image 20250527012241.png]]


Note if "App passwords" doesn't appear, your google account might not support App Password. If that's the case, you may wonder how to even go to "App passwords" - this is the link:
https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords

If you see that your account doesn't support App password, you need a different approach to authenticate such as OAuth2 to refresh token. You'd see this following screen.

In that case, you CANNOT follow [[2a. Gmail CLI with NodeMailer and App Password]]
Instead you can authenticate this way ([[1b. Authenticate with OAuth2 to Refresh Token]]) then try [[2b. Gmail CLI with NodeMailer and OAuth2 to Refresh Token]]
