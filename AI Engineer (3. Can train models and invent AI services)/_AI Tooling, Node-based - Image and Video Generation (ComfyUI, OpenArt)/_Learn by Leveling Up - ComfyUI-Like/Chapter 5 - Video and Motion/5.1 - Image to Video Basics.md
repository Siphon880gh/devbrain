**Platform note:** CUDA required for most img2vid templates — use **RunComfy** unless you have NVIDIA GPU with ≥12GB VRAM.

---

## Learning objectives

- Feed Chapter 4 hero image into an img2vid workflow.
- Tune **motion strength** / conditioning so subject stays recognizable.
- Export MP4/WebM suitable for Reels/TikTok upload.

---

## Steps

1. Open your chosen **SVD or img2vid** template on RunComfy (or local CUDA).
2. Upload hero still — match training resolution if template specifies.
3. Set **frame count** low (14–25) for first success.
4. Motion prompt (if node exists): subtle — `slow camera push, hair moves slightly, cinematic`
5. Queue; wait full run — do not interrupt.
6. Preview; if face warps, reduce motion strength / denoise / augmentation sliders per template docs.

---

## Motion tuning guide

| Symptom | Adjustment |
|---------|------------|
| Subject melts | Lower motion strength |
| Static slideshow | Slightly higher strength or camera motion words |
| Flicker | More frames at lower motion; check consistent seed |
| Wrong aspect | Re-crop still before upload |

---

## Post in editor

- Trim to **2–3 s** hook loop
- Add caption and sound in CapCut/DaVinci — ComfyUI clip is visual plate

---

## Beginner mistakes

- Portrait still → landscape video template without crop
- Expecting lip-sync dialogue from img2vid — needs different stack
- Leaving RunComfy GPU running after export

---

## Project checkpoint

**One 2–5 s clip** from your best still, recognizable subject, exported MP4.
