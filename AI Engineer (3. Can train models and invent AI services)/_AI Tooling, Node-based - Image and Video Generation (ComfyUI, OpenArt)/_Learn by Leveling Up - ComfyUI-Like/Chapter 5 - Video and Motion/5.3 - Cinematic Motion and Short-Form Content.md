**Platform note:** CUDA required — combine prior video lesson with editing toolchain.

---

## Learning objectives

- Design **short-form narrative**: hook still → motion reveal → CTA frame.
- Apply simple **camera language** in prompts (dolly, pan, orbit).
- Ship one vertical video piece using ComfyUI + editor.

---

## Short-form pipeline

```
Hero still (Ch.4) → img2vid (Ch.5.1) → trim/grade/caption (editor) → upload
```

Optional: txt2vid b-roll under voiceover ([[5.2 - Text to Video Starters]]).

---

## Camera words that work

| Term | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Slow dolly in | Intensity on subject |
| Orbit around subject | Product/character showcase |
| Static + ambient motion | Loops, wallpapers |
| Rack focus (if supported) | Cinematic feel — model dependent |

One camera move per clip while learning.

---

## Cinematic prompt add-ons

- `anamorphic lens flare` (subtle)
- `film grain, soft bloom`
- `volumetric light`

Do not stack with heavy LoRA simultaneously on first cinematic tests.

---

## Editor checklist

- [ ] 9:16 crop safe zones
- [ ] First frame readable at thumbnail size
- [ ] Loop point if TikTok loop bait
- [ ] Sound design — even simple whoosh on reveal

## Chapter 5 capstone

**One vertical short** (15–30 s total) combining:

- Hook still or before/after from [[4.3 - Viral Hook Templates and Before-After Formats]]
- 2–5 s img2vid motion
- Captions and music in external editor

## Beginner mistakes

- 30 s of uncut img2vid morphing
- No hook in frame 1
- Overgrading — crushed blacks hide detail
