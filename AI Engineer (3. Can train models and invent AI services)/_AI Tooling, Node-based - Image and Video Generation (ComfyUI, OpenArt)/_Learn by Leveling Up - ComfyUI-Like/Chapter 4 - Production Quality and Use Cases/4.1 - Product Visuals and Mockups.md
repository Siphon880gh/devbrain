**Platform note:** Stable for most computers — product still life txt2img + optional ControlNet depth.

---

## Learning objectives

- Prompt for **studio product photography** (single hero object, clean background).
- Combine img2img for label shape + inpaint for text-safe areas.
- Output shots usable for ecommerce mocks or ads.

---

## Prompt pattern

```
[product name/type], centered on [surface],
studio softbox lighting, soft shadow,
clean [color] background, commercial product photography,
sharp focus, high detail, no text
Negative: watermark, logo, misspelled text, cluttered background, hands
```

---

## Workflow options

### Pure txt2img

Fast concept mocks — bottle, gadget, packaging shape.

### Photo + img2img

Real product photo at low denoise for material retention; change background in prompt.

### Depth ControlNet

Keep object placement when swapping scene style.

---

## Steps

1. Generate 4 concepts — same aspect ratio as your shop or ad slot.
2. Pick one; inpaint label area if AI text gibberish (replace with blank or real composite in design tool).
3. Upscale winner ([[4.0 - Upscaling and Resolution Workflows]]).
4. Optional: batch 3 color variants via prompt swap only.

---

## Ethical / practical note

Do not fake real brand logos or regulated claims. Use generic packaging for learning.

---

## Beginner mistakes

- Asking model to render legible brand text — inpaint or overlay in Figma/Canva instead
- Busy backgrounds — product thumbnails need separation
- Wrong shadow direction vs composite plan

---

## Project checkpoint

**3 product hero images** same object family, upscaled, on clean backgrounds.
