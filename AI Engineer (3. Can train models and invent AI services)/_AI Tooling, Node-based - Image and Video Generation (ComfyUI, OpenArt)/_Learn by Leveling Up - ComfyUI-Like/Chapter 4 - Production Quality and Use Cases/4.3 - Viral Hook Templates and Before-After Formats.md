## Learning objectives

- Build **before/after** and **transformation** slides that stop the scroll.
- Pair still hooks with motion tease (setup for Chapter 5).
- Structure content for short-form narrative (problem → process → reveal).

---

## High-performing still formats

| Format | ComfyUI technique |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Before / after** | Same seed + img2img denoise sweep or LoRA off/on |
| **Glow-up** | Character consistency + outfit/background swap |
| **“AI vs reality” split** | Outpaint + composite layout in editor |
| **Process strip** | Save KSampler step previews if template exposes them |

---

## Before/after workflow

1. Generate **before** — flat light, muted prompt.
2. Duplicate graph; strengthen prompt + LoRA for **after**.
3. Optional: img2img from before at denoise 0.55 for coherent geometry.
4. Export both at **9:16** with space for caption top/bottom.

---

## Hook principles (first 1–2 seconds)

- High contrast subject vs background
- Single focal point — not cluttered
- Motion implied (hair, fabric, particles) even in still — helps transition to video

---

## Caption and post

ComfyUI delivers pixels; **hook text** often added in editor:

- Slide 1: question or bold claim
- Slide 2: mid transform
- Slide 3: final upscale from [[4.0 - Upscaling and Resolution Workflows]]

---

## Beginner mistakes

- Before/after with different seeds and unrelated compositions
- Too subtle a change — reads as duplicate slide
- No vertical safe zone for TikTok UI chrome
