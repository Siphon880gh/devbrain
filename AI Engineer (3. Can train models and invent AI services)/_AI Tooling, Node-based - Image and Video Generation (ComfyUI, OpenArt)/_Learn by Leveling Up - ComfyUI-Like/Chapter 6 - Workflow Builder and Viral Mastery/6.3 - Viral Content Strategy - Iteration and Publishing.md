## Learning objectives

- Turn workflows into a **content calendar**, not one-off experiments.
- Run **batch iteration** (seeds, hooks, formats) systematically.
- Measure what to automate in ComfyUI vs finish in editor.

---

## Content system

### Weekly rhythm (example)

| Day | ComfyUI focus | Output |
|-----|---------------|--------|
| Mon | Prompt + seed batch | 10 raw stills |
| Tue | Select + upscale + inpaint | 3 finals |
| Wed | img2vid on top 2 | 2 clips |
| Thu | Edit + caption | 2 posts scheduled |
| Fri | Review metrics; tune prompts | Updated preset card |

Adjust to your posting frequency.

---

## Iteration levers (in order)

1. **Hook still** — composition readable at thumbnail size
2. **Motion** — 2–3 s img2vid only on winners
3. **Format** — 9:16 first for TikTok/Reels
4. **Caption** — written for pause scroll, not describing image literally
5. **Series** — same LoRA/character builds audience memory

---

## What not to automate in ComfyUI

- Legible marketing copy (overlay in editor)
- Platform-specific safe zones
- A/B caption testing — use native analytics

---

## Batch queue tips

- Same workflow JSON; vary prompt CSV or manual queue
- Lock presets from [[2.3 - Tuning Seeds, Steps, CFG, and Samplers]]
- Archive seeds of top 10% outputs in a spreadsheet

---

## Beginner mistakes

- New workflow every post — no learnable feedback loop
- Publishing raw gens with artifact hands/faces
- Chasing trends without LoRA/brand consistency
