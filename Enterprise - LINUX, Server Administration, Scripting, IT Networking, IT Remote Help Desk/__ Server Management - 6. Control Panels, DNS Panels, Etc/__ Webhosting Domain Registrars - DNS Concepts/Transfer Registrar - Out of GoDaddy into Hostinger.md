Let's say we're moving to Namecheap. You will have to "transfer out" from GoDaddy to Namecheap. Thereafter, Namecheap will charge you yearly. You do have to pay for an initial year right away with Namecheap.

---

The process could take up to 7 days. It'll be faster if you do certain steps beforehand:
- Disable domain lock
	- Under "Domain Settings" on the DNS page at GoDaddy:
	  ![](43R1hAC.png)

- Disable privacy block

Additionally, ensure your domain is eligible for transfer by meeting the following criteria:
- The domain must have been registered or transferred at least 60 days ago.
- The domain must be unlocked at the current registrar.
- Privacy protection services should be disabled.
- Namecheap has a quick and easy transfer eligibility checker:
  https://www.namecheap.com/domains/transfer/
