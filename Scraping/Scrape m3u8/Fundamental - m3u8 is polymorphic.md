
An `.m3u8` file can be:
- **video only**
- **audio only**
- **video + audio together**
- **a list of different qualities**, like 360p, 720p, 1080p
- **a master file** that points to separate video/audio/quality playlists

So sometimes downloading one `.m3u8` gives you a normal video with sound.

Other times, the video and audio are separate, so downloading only one playlist may give you:
- video with no sound
- audio with a black screen
- only one quality level
